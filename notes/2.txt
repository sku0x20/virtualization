If you use a Type-1 Hypervisor (like Xen or bare-metal KVM) to boot Unikernels, then yes: the traditional General Purpose Operating System (GPOS) is gone.
You no longer have a "Host OS" with a desktop, a shell, or background cron jobs.
The Hypervisor becomes the "Thin OS," and your applications are the "Plugins."
